Emergency BoardUp

  • Human beings do not have control over natural disasters or some other certain situations in life, the only thing we can do is to limit the damage to the best of our abilities. Natural disasters such as Hail, storms, tornadoes, water damage, flood and fire outbreaks can happen to anyone at any time, be it a residential property or commercial institutions. Even man made disasters such as vehicle accidents can occur unpredictably. When tragic situations such as this happen the best thing to do is to try and limit the damages done and prevent further damages from happening. And this is where Emergency Board up services come in. What is Emergency Board Up? Emergency Board Up simply means services used to protect properties before and after repairs are done to the property in order to prevent further damages from various sources such as humans, elements and animals. Example of Emergency Board Up services are:

  • A tarp over the roof or chimney to prevent birds and other flying animals from occupying the property while still under repair.

  • Boarded up window to keep out rainfall from causing further damages to appliances that are not water resistant.

  • Boarded up doors to prevent animals, wild or not from making the property their homes before it becomes inhabited by humans again.

Advantages of Emergency BoardUp

Making repairs such as lock and door installation, repainting and other forms of repairs can take quite a long time, that is why we always encourage property owners to always board up during repair or after disasters. The following are some of the few advantages that emergency board up has:

  • Safety From Burglars:Burglars are always attracted to stealing from an empty house as there will be no one there to stop or arrest them. They will go away with stealing money, electronics and other valuables, leaving the property owners with an extra cost to replace what has been stolen. Therefore, it is always advisable for you to board up your house when in repair against unpermitted entry.


  • Safety From Animals: An open rooftop or door is an invitation to all sorts of pests and animals such as raccoons, squirrels, rats, birds and so on. These animals will urinate and defecate all over the house, tear up and chew anything chewable, trace dirt all over the house making the house inhabitable at the first glance. All of these will only cost the property owners extra cost to clean up the mess made and make the property habitable again. In order to avoid unnecessary costs and charges, the best thing you can do is to board up your property properly.


  • Safety From Natural Elements: A home that has been recently affected by natural disaster is already very frail and vulnerable structurally, causing further damages when the weather conditions become extreme, further damages will be done to the property if not properly boarded up. For example heavy rain or winds can penetrate openings like windows and doors in the building causing irreversible damage. The best way to prevent this kind of further disaster is to get professionals to help you perfectly board up your property.
  • Cost Reduction: A properly boarded property will not cost you extra charges, rather you will only focus on repairing the damages done and not extra damages done. Whenever you need to board up your property , be it residential or commercial contact Howards Lock and Key for the perfect emergency board up services.
